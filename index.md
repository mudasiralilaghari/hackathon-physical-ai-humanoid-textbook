# Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics: Complete Textbook

Welcome to the complete university-level textbook on **Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics**. This comprehensive resource bridges digital AI to real-world humanoid robotics, preparing students conceptually for ROS 2, simulation, and AI-robot systems.

## Learning Approach

Each chapter follows a consistent structure:
- **Short Introduction**: Context and objectives for the chapter
- **Clear Explanations**: Conceptual understanding with minimal heavy code
- **Learning Summary**: Key points and takeaways
- **Self-Assessment Questions**: Knowledge check and reflection prompts

## Target Audience

This textbook is designed for:
- Students with basic Python knowledge
- Beginners in robotics
- AI learners transitioning to Physical AI
- Anyone interested in understanding the fundamentals of humanoid robotics

## Prerequisites

- Basic programming concepts (especially Python)
- Fundamental mathematics (algebra and basic calculus)
- Interest in robotics and AI

## How to Use This Textbook

1. **Progressive Learning**: Read modules in order, as each builds on previous concepts
2. **Active Engagement**: Answer self-assessment questions after each chapter
3. **Practical Application**: Consider how concepts apply to real-world scenarios
4. **Supplementary Resources**: Explore the referenced tools and technologies
